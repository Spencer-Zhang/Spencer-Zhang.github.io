My values

April 24, 2014

My most important values are Knowledge, Challenge, and Growth

Of those three, Knowledge, or Understanding, is the value that is most important to me. In the past, I've always felt the most accomplished when I'm learning something new, or when I feel like I've gained a deeper understanding of something. In programming, in drawing, and in a lot of my activities, I've had a lot of moments where I would notice a pattern in what I was doing, and that would consequently help me understand my subject better.

In general, I try to live up to those values:

* Knowledge: 5 (Strongly Agree)
* Challenge: 4 (Agree)
* Growth: 4 (Agree)

The last thing someone asked me for help on is advice on how to draw. I'm not a very good artist myself, but I think I'm decent at giving critiques and feedback.
